LeafWatch

Plant Health Monitoring System

Project Overview

LeafWatch is an app that helps users monitor the health of their plants using machine learning. It identifies plant diseases based on leaf images and provides recommendations for care and treatment.

Key features include real-time disease detection, a database of common plant diseases, and personalized care tips. The app also offers a history log to track the health status of plants over time.

Developing LeafWatch allowed me to explore the integration of machine learning with mobile applications, particularly in the domain of agricultural technology.

Tech Stack: Flutter, Firebase, Firestore, Machine Learning, Flask, LLM
