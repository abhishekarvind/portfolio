SolarScope

Solar Energy Monitoring & Management

Project Overview

Solar Scope is an innovative solution designed to monitor and manage solar energy systems. It allows users to track real-time data on energy production, storage, and consumption.

Key features include energy output tracking, efficiency analysis, and notifications for maintenance or potential issues. The app also provides detailed insights into solar energy utilization over time.

Developing Solar Scope provided experience in working with IoT devices, real-time data analytics, and cloud-based data storage, making it a comprehensive project in renewable energy technology.

Tech Stack: Flutter, Firebase, Firestore,Flask
