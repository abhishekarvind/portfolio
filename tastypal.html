TastyPal

Food Discovery & Recommendation App

Project Overview

TastyPal is a food discovery app designed to help users find nearby restaurants, explore popular dishes, and discover new culinary experiences. It offers personalized recommendations based on user preferences.

Features include a restaurant finder, dish reviews, personalized recommendations, and a favorites section to save preferred dishes and eateries.

Building TastyPal involved integrating various APIs and ensuring real-time data synchronization, providing valuable experience in user experience design and data management.

Tech Stack: Flutter, Firebase, Firestore, Google Places API, Flask, LLM
